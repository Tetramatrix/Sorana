What is Sorana?

Sorana is an AI Visual Workspace that converts static folders into a dynamic, visual environment. It's designed to organize digital chaos by combining spatial layouts with powerful no-code AI agent building capabilities.

Core Capabilities

  • Spatial AI Organization: Sorana uses AI to semantically group your assets and arranges them on an intelligent 2D canvas, providing an intuitive, bird's-eye view of your projects.
  • WYSIWYG Canvas Editor: Directly manipulate your workspace. Drag and drop files between groups, create new categories on the fly, rename items, and resize group boundaries with intuitive mouse controls.
  • Advanced Model Management: Connect to multiple cloud and local LLM backends including OpenAI, Mistral, Ollama, and Lemonade. The model manager lists all available models and allows users to activate or deactivate models as needed for specific tasks.
  • No-Code Agent Orchestration: Build custom agents and connect them into intelligent pipelines using a simple drag-and-drop interface. Agents collaborate by passing insights from one to another to solve complex problems, all without writing code.
  • Contextual Document Chat: Interact directly with your files (PDFs, code, text) in interactive mode, and enhance agent capabilities by connecting relevant documents to their context.
  • Dynamic Structure Mapping: Visualize the big picture. Generate mind maps of your folder hierarchies to reveal relationships and structure.
  • Private & Portable: Run AI models locally (Llama, Ollama) or connect to the cloud. Sorana is fully portable and designed to keep your data under your control.

Who is Sorana For?

Sorana is designed for knowledge workers, researchers, developers, and anyone who needs to organize and make sense of complex digital information:

  • Developers: Organize codebases, document projects, and build AI workflows. Visualize codebase structure and document APIs.
  • Researchers: Manage research papers, datasets, and notes with intelligent spatial organization and document chat.
  • Professionals: Keep project files, documents, and resources organized and accessible with visual mind mapping. Analyze documents and build automated data processing pipelines.
  • Creatives: Organize digital assets and project files in an intuitive visual workspace.
  • AI Enthusiasts: Build and experiment with AI agent pipelines using a no-code interface.

AI: How It Works, Fine-Tuning & Manual Fallback

Why are my results "hit-or-miss"?

Sorana uses AI models to group and arrange icons. The default model is lightweight and works offline, but may not always classify every icon perfectly, especially for less common applications. Advanced users can connect larger or custom models for improved accuracy, but this may require more system resources.

  • Offline & Free (Default): The built-in model (~806MB) is downloaded on first run and works fully offline. It is fast, but may sometimes mislabel complex icons as "Miscellaneous."
  • Custom Models: For improved results, Sorana can connect to local services (like Ollama or Llama), OpenAI-compatible servers, or download larger models from Hugging Face.

Note: Larger models can provide better grouping, but require more memory and processing power.

Manual Fine-Tuning & Fallback

If the AI does not classify an icon as desired, or if no model is available, you can adjust the results manually using intiutive drag and drap and rename groups.
